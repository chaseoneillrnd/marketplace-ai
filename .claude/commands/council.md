---
description: "Council of Experts — multi-agent parallel discussion, debate, or collaborative improvement"
---

Invoke the `council` skill with the user's full input as the task. Pass along everything after `/council` as the task argument.

The user may say things like:
- `/council debate how the current testing strategy compares against XYZ`
- `/council bring 6 experts to discuss the best architecture for billing`
- `/council 3 prompt-engineering experts improve this prompt to do XYZ`
- `/council discuss why the pipeline is broken`
- `/council benefitly-expert brainstorming rag-pipeline -- should we add voice?`
